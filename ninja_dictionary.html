Ninja

ninja (noun) - a skilled and covert mercenary or assassin in feudal Japan.

Attributes

  1. Stealth: Ninjas are known for their ability to move silently and remain undetected.
  2. Combat: They are highly trained in various forms of martial arts and combat techniques.
  3. Weaponry: Ninjas are skilled in using a wide array of weapons, including swords, shurikens, and more.
  4. Infiltration: They excel in infiltrating enemy territories and gathering valuable information.
